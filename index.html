Mobile Development

There is a big demand for mobile development because of the large and medium sized companies are increasingly adapting to the mobile world in this rapidly and technologically changing society. It is an introductory course to investigate the different mobile architectures (native, web app and cross-platform or hybrid) on various platforms with examples. The course introduces the students the concepts, tools and techniques used to develop quality mobile apps for diverse platforms and devices, including Apple iOS, Android, and Windows with examples covering all the different architectures. The students will create simple mobile apps covering native, web app and hybrid architectures using easily available tools.
